Design & Build
AD3London

Residential & Commercial D&B

We understand that deciding how your living or business space will look like and bringing it into reality is an exciting and sometimes a life changing journey. Your project might be small, like turning a bathroom into a sanctuary or hallway into an office, while on the other side of the spectrum, you are raising the roof (quite literally), extending the house or working on the strip out refurbishment of a hotel or a restaurant.
Every project is unique as are your requirements. For that very reason our Design & Build services are split into stages so you can easily decide how much you’d like us to be involved. You can choose AD3 to work on your project’s design stage, build stage or both.

For smaller projects with restricted funding you might need our assistance with design, recommendation of trade professionals and bravely manage the project yourself or hire someone else to do it. For an even bigger saving you can use our Online Design Service.

If, on the other hand, dealing with countless trade professionals is the stuff of your nightmares and you just want to walk in once it is all over, we’ll manage your project and put your mind at ease with the clear and structured project plan, regular reports and staged payments - a process that generates mutual trust and safeguards all parties involved.

AD3 Design & Build collaborative offers design and build services under one umbrella, simplifying the process of choosing suitable professionals for your project.

Our fees are very competitive, their structure is simple and tailored to your project. Balancing yours, design and build teams’ requirements and responsibilities, Project Manager is integral to our team and their fees are covered within the build price once the building work commences.

If you have a project in mind and need help with it, get in touch - we’ll help you fall in love with it. We design and create exceptional and considered spaces tailored to your desired lifestyle, financial abilities and your personal design flair. We strive to integrate sustainable solutions wherever possible including both design and build stages.
